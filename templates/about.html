About GIS Crowd Prediction

What It Does

This project uses satellite imagery and environmental data such as NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) and temperature, combined with machine learning models, to estimate crowd density at specific locations.

Why Monitor Overcrowding?

  • Overcrowding can lead to safety hazards including stampedes and accidents.
  • It strains public infrastructure and emergency services.
  • High crowd density can accelerate the spread of infectious diseases.
  • Monitoring helps city planners and event organizers manage crowds effectively.

Impact of This Project

By providing accurate crowd density predictions, this tool supports:

  • Improved public safety and emergency response.
  • Better urban planning and infrastructure development.
  • Enhanced event management and crowd control.
